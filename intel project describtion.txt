
To create dashboards and visualizations that help Intel's Sustainability Team select the optimal location for a new data center based on energy availability, usage, and reliance on renewable energy sources.
1:Net Production: Identify regions that are net energy producers through visualization.
2:Supply and Demand by Region: Visualize fluctuations in energy production and demand over time
3:Renewable Energy: which regions generate the highest percentage of renewable energy.
4:Utility Power Source Breakdown: illustrate the energy source composition by region and balancing authority.
5:Hourly Difference in Generation:display percent differences in energy generation by hour.
6:Energy Source By Region:depicting the various sources of energy per region over a selected period.
